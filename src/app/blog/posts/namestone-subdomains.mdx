Managing subdomains on your Ethereum Name Service (ENS) domain is easy with Namestone. Here's how you can add subdomains:

1. Go to [Namestone](https://namestone.io) and connect your wallet.
2. Select your ENS domain from the dashboard.
3. Click on "Add Subdomain" and enter the desired subdomain name.
4. Confirm the transaction in your wallet.
5. Your subdomain will be live after the transaction is confirmed.

_Note: You can manage permissions and set records for each subdomain individually._
